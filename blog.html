SOFT ROBOTICS

ROBOTICS is a huge branch in itself. Each of its branches have proved to be a great topic for the researchers. One of its sub-branches is Soft-Robotics. This field deals with creating robots that are made up of or some of their parts are made up of complaint(flexible) materials. These robots ensure more flexibility, adaptability for accomplishing tasks, as well as improved safety when working around humans. Hence they are used in medicine and manufacturing.

SENSORS

We all know that sensors are one of the most useful technologies that play an important role in robotics. To navigate the real world and interact with physical objects robots need to sense the world around them. Here the role of sensors comes into play. Sensors can be either internal or external. Internal sensors tell the information about the robot itself.
